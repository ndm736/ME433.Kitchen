## Suantang Feiniu (Sour Soup Beef Slices)

Make sure to serve it hot with rice.

### Ingredients
- 300 g thinly sliced beef
- 1 pack enoki mushrooms
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 2 green chili peppers
- 2 red chili peppers
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tablespoon ginger, sliced
- 2 tablespoons yellow lantern chili sauce
- 3 tablespoons cooking oil
- 2 cups chicken broth or water
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cooking wine
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 handful chopped cilantro
- 1 green onion, chopped

### Instructions
1. Wash the enoki mushrooms and bean sprouts, then place them in a large bowl.
2. Heat water in a pot and quickly blanch the beef slices until they just change color. Remove and set aside.
3. Heat oil in a pan, then add the garlic, ginger, and yellow lantern chili sauce. Stir-fry until fragrant.
4. Pour in the chicken broth or water and bring it to a boil.
5. Add soy sauce, cooking wine, salt, white pepper, and vinegar. Stir well.
6. Add the enoki mushrooms and bean sprouts, and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Transfer the vegetables and soup into a serving bowl, then place the beef slices on top.
8. Add the chopped green and red chili peppers, cilantro, and green onion over the beef.
9. Heat a small amount of oil and pour it over the toppings for extra flavor.

Enjoy!
