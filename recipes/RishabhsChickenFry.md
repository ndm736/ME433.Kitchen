 
# Easy Indian Chicken Fry

## Ingredients
-1 lb. boneless chicken – cut into 1.5 inches cubes
-1/2 tsp turmeric powder
-1 tsp salt
-1 tsp cumin powder
-1 tsp chili powder
-1 tsp coriander powder
-1 tsp garam masala
-1.5 tbsp plain yogurt
-1 tbsp lime juice
-1 tsp ground ginger paste
-1 tsp ground garlic paste
-3 tbsp oil
-1⁄4 cup chopped cilantro leaves
## Instructions
1. Mix all dry spices, yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste and 1 tbsp of oil in a bowl
2. Marinate the cut chicken in the bowl for at least half an hour
3. Heat a pan and add the remaining oil
4. Slowly add the chicken. Watch out since it will splatter
5. Saute the chicken in medium heat for 5 minutes. Increase the heat Keep stirring (about 10 to 15
minutes) till the chicken is cooked. The chicken should be dry and slightly browned and crispy
6. Remove from heat. Sprinkle cut cilantro leaves
