## Sri Lankan Hoppers Recipe
<!---

The ultimate Sri Lankan food that everyone loves!

### Ingredients
- 200 g rice flour
- 1 pinch of yeast
- 3/4 cup of water
- 200 ml of coconut milk
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp salt
- Coconut oil

### Instructions
1. Make a dough-like paste using rice flour, yeast, and water.
2. Cover it and let it ferment for 18-20 hours.
3. Add sugar and coconut milk to make a somewhat thick batter.
4. Cover again and let it sit for 30 minutes.
5. Add salt to the batter and mix until you get a homogeneous mixture.
6. Heat the hopper pan on medium heat,


Enjoy!
