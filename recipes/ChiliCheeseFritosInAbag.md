## Nikolai Harris'

This is one of my favorite struggle meals, offering nearly no nutritional benefit but it makes the hunger leave

### Ingredients
- Chili Cheese Fritos
- Onions
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Cheese
- Guacamole 

### Instructions
1. Open bag of chips
2. Season the chips with both powders and mix in with onions
3. Only after those ingredients are mixed, add cheese then guacamole so as not to make the chips too wet

Enjoy!
