## Soy Honey Glazed Salmon
This is a quick meal prep I do to just throw into the oven and wait

## Ingredients
* 1 cup of soy sauce
* 1 cup of honey
* 2 tablespoons of garlic
* 2 tablespoons of seasame oil
* Pinch of black pepper
* 1 cup of water
* 6 Salmon Fillets
* (optional) 1 Egg
* (optional) Bowl of Rice

## Instructions
1. Mix up all your ingredients for your glaze
2. Place salmon into the glaze
3. Put salmon and glaze combination into the refrigerator and marinate for 10 minutes
4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
5. Line a tray with a baking sheet and place salmon side-by-side
6. Put salmon into the oven for 10-15 minutes
7. Take tray out and rest salmon for 3 minutes
8. Fry an egg
9. In a bowl, put in rice then salmon then fried egg
10. Enjoy!
