# What Are We Making?
Welcome to my recipe on **Fujianese Ban Mian**. This is one of my favorite meals from my childhood and a comfort meal! The cooking and prep time combined is around 10 minutes.

## Ingredients

To make this meal it is very simple! There is only a couple ingredients!

- For the Sauce
    - 2 Tbsp Creamy Peanut Butter
    - 1 Tsp Sesame Oil
    - 1 or 2 Tsp Oyster Sauce (Adjust according to preference)
    - 2 Tbsp Regular Soy Sauce
    - 1 Tsp Sesame Sauce (Optional)
    - 2 Cloves Minced Garlic (Optional)

- For the Noodles
    - 2 Servings of Thick Wonton Noodles
    - 1 Scallion Chopped
    - Chili Oil/Sauce (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring pot of water to boil and add noodles and cook according to package instructions
2. While the noodles are cooking, add sauce ingredients into a bowl and mix thoroughly
3. Add about 3 or 4 Tbsp of the noodle water to the sauce to get it to your desired consistency
4. Drain the noodles, then place them onto a large dish. Mix thouroughly with the sauce and add more noodle water as needed until fully incorporated. Top with chopped scallions and optional chili oil/sauce and enjoy!
