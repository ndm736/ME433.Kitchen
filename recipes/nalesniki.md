## Naleśniki
Polish take on crepes

### Ingredients
- all-purpose flour
- eggs
- milk
- melted butter or vegetable oil
- sugar
- salt
- (optional toppings: nutella, honey, fruits, jam)

### Instructions
1. Stir 1 cup of flour, 1.5 cups of milk, 2 eggs, 1 tbsp sugar, and a pinch of salt
2. Add oil / melt butter on a medium heat pan
3. Pour mix onto pan keeping it thin
4. Flip when brown
5. Add toppings and enjoy
