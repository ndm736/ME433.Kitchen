# Baked Boursin Pasta

Made this once and it turned out pretty good.

## Ingredients

* 2 Packages of Boursin Cheese - Garlic & Herb Preferred
* 2 Pints Cherry Tomatos
* 1 Diced Medium Yellow Onion
* 4 Minced Garlic Cloves
* 4 Tablespoons Olive Oil
* 16 oz Pasta - Penne Preferred
* 1/4 cup Fresh Basil
* 1 cup Spinach
* Salt & Pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
1. In a medium baking dish add Boursin, diced onion, minced garlic, and cherry tomatos. Drizzle with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper.
1. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
1. While baking cook pasta al dente and save 1/2 cup of pasta water.
1. Remove dish from the oven, then add pasta and pasta water and mix all together with basil and spinach.
1. Enjoy!
