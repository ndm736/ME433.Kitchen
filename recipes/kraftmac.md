## Microwave Kraft Mac and Cheese
<!---
Put your name or github username somewhere
-->
Ethan Chien loves kraft mac and cheese so why not share that love with the world

### Ingredients
- 6 cups water
- 1 package Kraft mac and cheese
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup milk

### Instructions
1. Bring water to boil in medium pot. Add mac, cook for 7 to 8 minutes, stirring occasionally
2. Drain and put mac in bowl.
3. Add butter, milk, and cheese sauce mix. Mix well.

Enjoy!
