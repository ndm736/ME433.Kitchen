# Veggie Maggi 🍜

A quick, delicious, and wholesome twist on the classic Maggi noodles — loaded with colorful vegetables and aromatic spices.

[Jump to Instructions](#instructions) | [Tips & Tricks](#tips)

There should be whitespace between paragraphs.

Veggie Maggi is one of the most beloved comfort foods in South Asia. It's **fast**, **flexible**, and endlessly customizable. Whether you're a student, a busy professional, or just craving something warm — this recipe has you covered.

---

## Ingredients

| Item                  | Quantity          | Notes                  |
|:----------------------|:------------------|:-----------------------|
| Maggi noodles         | 2 packets         | With tastemaker        |
| Water                 | 1.5 cups          | Per packet             |
| Onion                 | 1 medium          | Finely chopped         |
| Tomato                | 1 medium          | Chopped                |
| Carrot                | 1 small           | Diced or grated        |
| Capsicum              | ½                 | Any color, diced       |
| Green peas            | ¼ cup             | Fresh or frozen        |
| Green chilli          | 1–2               | Slit, adjust to taste  |
| Ginger-garlic paste   | 1 tsp             | Optional but recommended |
| Oil or butter         | 1 tbsp            | Butter adds richness   |
| Salt                  | To taste          |                        |
| Fresh coriander       | A handful         | For garnish            |

---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Prep Your Veggies

*   Chop the **onion**, **tomato**, and **capsicum** into small, even pieces.
*   Dice or grate the **carrot** so it cooks quickly.
*   Slit the **green chillies** lengthwise.
*   Keep your **green peas** ready (thaw if frozen).

### Step 2 — Sauté the Base

1.  Heat **1 tbsp oil or butter** in a pan over medium flame.
2.  Add the green chillies and sauté for 30 seconds.
3.  Add the chopped **onions** and cook until golden and translucent.
4.  Stir in the **ginger-garlic paste** and cook for another minute.

### Step 3 — Add the Vegetables

1.  Toss in the **carrots**, **capsicum**, and **green peas**.
2.  Sauté on medium-high heat for **2–3 minutes**.
3.  Add the **tomatoes** and cook until they soften.
4.  Season lightly with **salt** at this stage.

### Step 4 — Cook the Noodles

> This is the most important step — getting the water ratio right is key to perfectly cooked Maggi.
>
> Too much water makes it soggy; too little leaves it undercooked.

1.  Pour in **1.5 cups of water per packet** used.
2.  Bring to a **rolling boil**.
3.  Break the Maggi cakes and add them to the pan.
4.  Sprinkle in the **tastemaker sachet(s)**.
5.  Stir gently and cook for **2 minutes**, stirring occasionally.

### Step 5 — Finish & Serve

*   Turn off the heat while the noodles still look slightly wet — they'll absorb the remaining moisture.
*   Garnish generously with **fresh coriander**.
*   Serve immediately in a bowl or straight from the pan!

## Variations

### Cheesy Veggie Maggi

Add a slice of **processed cheese** or a sprinkle of **grated cheddar** on top while the Maggi is still hot. Cover for 30 seconds to melt.

### Egg Veggie Maggi

Crack an **egg** directly into the pan just before adding noodles. Scramble it into the veggie base for extra protein.

### Masala Upgrade

```
Extra spices you can add to the sauté step:
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp cumin seeds
- ½ tsp red chilli powder
- A pinch of garam masala
```

## Tips

<dl>
<dt>Don't overcook</dt>
<dd>Maggi turns mushy fast. Remove from heat when it's still slightly al dente.</dd>
<dt>Butter > Oil</dt>
<dd>Using butter instead of oil takes the flavor up a notch significantly.</dd>
<dt>Veggie size matters</dt>
<dd>Chop veggies small and uniformly so they cook in the same time as the noodles.</dd>
<dt>Water control</dt>
<dd>If you like dry Maggi, use less water. For a soupy version, add an extra half cup.</dd>
</dl>

### And a quick recap of the process:

- Prep veggies
  - Chop onion, tomato, capsicum
  - Dice or grate carrot
    - Smaller pieces cook faster
    - Uniform size = even cooking
  - Ready the peas
- Cook the base
  - Sauté aromatics
  - Add vegetables
- Boil & mix noodles
  - Add tastemaker
  - Stir and cook 2 minutes
- Garnish and serve
