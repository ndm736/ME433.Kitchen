## Tony Shilati's Chelokebab Recipe

This a a Persian meal that my dad makes for my family. 

### Ingredients
#### Kebab
*   0.5 kg of ground lamb or beef
*   1 large onion
*   1 tsp salt
*   1/2 tsp black pepper
*   1/4 tsp turmeric
*   1/4 tsp sumac

#### Rice
*   2 cups of basmati rice
*   salt
*   2 tsp butter
*   A pinch of saffron


### Instructions
1. Mix the kebab meat and add seasoning 
2. Grill the kebabs
3. Make the rice
