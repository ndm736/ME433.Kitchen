## Cumin Lamb

Really yummy and tasty lamb recipe made by my mom. 

### Ingredients

*   Lamb
*   Soy sauce
*   Dried cumin spice
*   Chili powder
*   Red pepper powder
*   Corn starch
*   Onions

### Instructions
1.  Slice the lamb into pieces
2.  Chop up the onions into pieces
3.  Marinate lamb in a bowl with soy sauce, dried cumin spice, chili powder, and corn starch (add as much as you like)
4.  Preheat the pan of choice until it's hot
5.  Add oil and lamb into pan
6.  Add salt and red pepper powder onto the lamb
7.  Stir fry the lamb until the insides are not red. Flip each piece so both sides are cooked
8.  Remove lamb from the pan (put it onto a plate or something), then add the onions
9.  Stir fry the onions (no strict cooking time, just go with the feel)
10. Remove the onions from the pan and combine it with the lamb

Enjoy!
