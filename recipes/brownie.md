## Brownies

I love brownies.

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, or oil
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

### Instructions
1. Pre-heat oven to 350F. Grease an 8x8 square pan or a 9x5 loaf pan or line with parchment or foil; set aside.
2. In a large microwave-safe mixing bowl, add the butter and microwave for 1 minute or until completely melted. Add the sugar and cocoa powder and whisk vigorously for at least a minute, until the sugar has dissolved.
3. Add eggs one at a time then vanilla and whisk for at least 1 minute, until well combined and batter is shiny.
4. Stir in flour and salt until the flour is fully combined. Be careful not to overmix mix. Add chocolate chips
5. Spread in pan and bake for approximately 20 minutes or until the center is slightly set.
6. Cool completely and cut into 4 triangular pieces and 1 circle.
