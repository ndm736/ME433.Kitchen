## Manash's Stir Fry Order

Plex Stir Fry is my favourite dining hall food but making it requires immense skill which I will now bless you with!

### Ingredients
- 1 spoon of Garlic
- 1 generous serving of spring onions
- 8-10 Bamboo Shoot pieces (layer the bottom of the bowl)
- 1/2 cup of mushrooms (fill the bowl)
- 2 spoons of carrots
- as much bell pepper as you can fit

### Instructions
1. Wait patiently in the long stir fry line
2. Rapidly fill your bowl with the above ingredients
3. Greet the chef since they've had a long day 
4. Hand them the bowl and quickly say just noodles (I'm vegetarian)
5. Wait eagerly for the stir fry to be prepared
6. Pick up the stir fry and drizzle it with hot sauce

Enjoy! (if you have a dining plan)
