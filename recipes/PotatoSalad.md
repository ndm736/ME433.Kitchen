## Karl's Potato Salad

### Ingredients
- 4 potatoes
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon mustard
- 2 tablespoons chopped onion
- Salt and pepper

### Steps
1. Boil the potatoes until tender, then let them cool and chop them.
2. Mix the mayonnaise, mustard, onion, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
3. Stir in the potatoes, chill if you like, and serve.
