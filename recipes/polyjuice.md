## Jacob's Polyjuice Potion
<!---
Put your name or github username somewhere
-->
Adapted from [https://harrypotter.fandom.com/wiki/Polyjuice_Potion]

### Instruments
- Copper cauldron (for maximum brewing speed)
- Pestle and mortar
- Wand

### Ingredients

- 3 measures of fluxweed, picked on full moon
- 2 bundles of knotgrass
- 4 leeches
- 3 scoops of lacewing flies
- 3 measures of boomslang skin
- 1 measure of bicorn horn
- 1 piece of the user you wish to impersonate, such as hair

### Instructions
## Part 0
1. Stew lacewing flies for 21 days.

## Part 1
1. Add fluxweed to the cauldron.
2. Add knotgrass to the cauldron.
3. Stir 4 times clockwise.
4. Wave your wand and brew for 60 minutes.
5. Add leeches to the cauldron.
6. Grind up 2 scoops of lacewing flies.
7. Add 2 measures of crushed lacewing flies to the cauldron.
8. Apply low heat for 30 seconds.
9. Wave your wand to finish.

## Part 2
1. Add boomslang skin to the cauldron.
2. Grind up 1 measure of bicorn horn and add it to the cauldron.
3. Apply high heat for 20 seconds.
4. Wave your wand and and brew for 18 hours.
5. Add 1 scoop of lacewing flies.
6. Stir 3 times counterclockwise.
7. Add the piece of the user to be impersonated.
8. Wave your wand to finish.

Enjoy!
