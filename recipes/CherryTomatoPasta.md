## Cherry Tomato Pasta
<!---
kyuwonweon
-->

Pasta recipe with fresh cherry tomato sauce

### Ingredients
- olive oil 
- pepper
- cherry tomatoes
- linguine
- basil 
- salt 
- crushed pepper
- parmesan cheese

### Instructions
1. Put water in a pot and boil pasta
2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add chopped garlic and stir
3. Put cherry tomatoes, salt, and pepper and cook for 7-8 minutes until tomatoes start to burst
4. Add crushed pepper, fresh basil and cook pasta and stir 
5. Serve pasta with shredded Parmesan cheese
