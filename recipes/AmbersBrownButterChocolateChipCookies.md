## Amber's Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

### Wet Ingredients
- 2 sticks unsalted butter
- 1 cup dark brown sugar, packed
- 1/3 cup white sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

### Dry Ingredients
- 2 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1-2 tsp espresso powder
- Chocolate chips (whatever kind/amount you prefer)

### Instructions
1. Mix the sugars together until combined.
2. Put the butter in a pan on low-medium heat and stir until brown. Let it cool off before adding it to the sugar. Mix on high for about 1 minute.
3. Add eggs and vanilla extract and mix on medium-high for about 2 minutes or until color turns lighter.
4. Put all dry ingredients besides chocolate chips in a bowl and whisk them together until combined.
5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients incrementally while mixing on low. Once just combined, add chocolate chips and fold them in.
6. Rest dough for an hour minimum.
7. Bake at 375 degrees for about 10-14 minutes or whenever the edges of the cookies are golden brown.
8. Enjoy!
