## Smoothie Bowl Recipe

You will need the following items:
- two to three fruits that you like (person preference to choose fruits of compatiable colo groups)
    - choose two very flavorful fruits (like bananas, blueberries, or mangos) and one watery fruit (pears, apples, grapes) to help mixture
- aloe vera juice
- oats
- chia seeds
- yougurt of choice 
- semi sweet chocolate chips
- granola
- bowl
- smoothie blender

Recipe:
1. chop fruit such that each fruit is 1/3 of the blender approximately (freestyle if you wish) and one fruit of choice has extra for topping at the end
2. add blender fruit and aloe vera juice until the container is a 1/3 full with juice **with** the fruit in it
3. blend
4. add oats until the blender is full and blend again
5. pour smoothie into the bowl, add chia seeds and mix
6. add fruit, yoghurt, chocolate chips, an gronola on top in quadrants

Now enjoy!
