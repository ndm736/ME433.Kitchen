## Roasted Broccoli
<!---
Put your name or github username somewhere
username: kgpartee
-->

This broccoli is the perfect side to any meal!

### Ingredients
- 2 heads of broccoli
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- grated parmesan, optional 

### Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees
2. Cut the broccoli into bite sized florets
3. Spread the broccoli into an even layer on a baking sheet
4. Drizzle the olive oil and lemon juice over the broccoli and toss to coat
5. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the broccoli
6. bake for 20-25 minutes or until edges begin to brown
7. if desired, remove after 15 minutes and sprinkle parmesan over top of the broccoli, then put back in the oven until cooked

Enjoy!
