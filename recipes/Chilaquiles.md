## Chilaquiles
<!---
Put your name or github username somewhere
Tiffany Sorto
-->
Breakfast

### Ingredients (about 6 servings)
- 6 tomatoes
- 4 jalapenos (or more if you like it spicy)
- 3 garlic cloves
- monterey jack cheese
- corn tortillas
- onion
- eggs
- sour cream
- chicken bouillon
- vegetable oil

### Instructions
1. Cut tortilla chips into quarters and fry in vegetable oil. set on a napkin to dry excess oil
2. Roast tomatoes, jalapenos, and garlic until charred 
3. Blend all together and add salt, chicken bouillon and a small amount of water to bring to a saucy consistency
4. Shred cheese
5. Cut onions small
6. Set up chips in a baking dish, sprinkle onion and cheese, pour sauce all over
7. Bake at 350 for about 15 min
8. Cook eggs as desired
9. Serve with sour cream

Enjoy!
