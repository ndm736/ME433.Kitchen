# Vanilla Frosty (using Ninja Creami Machine)


### Ingredients
*   1 Cup Any Type Milk
*   1/2 Cup Water
*   1/4 tsp Salt
*   1/4 tsp Xantham Gum
*   1 tsp Vanilla Extract (or measure with your heart)
*   2.5 Tbsp Monk Fruit Sweetener

### Recipe
1.  Throw all ingredients into machine provided container
2.  Use a frother to combine well
3.  Freese the mixer for 24hr or overnight
4.  Run the container under hot water for 15 seconds
5.  Blend on the light ice cream setting
6.  Scrape the edges in the container to mix and rerun on the rerun setting
7.  Add a splash of milk and vanilla extract into the mixer and rerun on mix in setting

Enjoy!!!
