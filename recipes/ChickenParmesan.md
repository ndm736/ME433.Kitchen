## Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan is an italian chicken dish with a delicous, cheesy sauce. It's easy to make and can be served with pasta for a complete italian meal. 

### Ingredients (4 servings)
- 4  (5- to 6-oz.) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- ½ tsp. black pepper
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 cups panko bread crumbs
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese (3 oz.)
- 2 Tbsp. flour
- ¾ cup olive oil
- 1⅓ cups marinara sauce
- 2½ oz. fresh mozzarella, cut into ¼-inch cubes (½ cup)
- ½ cup shredded provolone cheese (2 oz.)
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
2. Put chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic on a work surface. 
3. Pound chicken with smooth side of a meat mallet or rolling pin into ½-inch-thick cutlets. 
4. Sprinkle with pepper.
5. Add eggs to a shallow dish. In another shallow dish, stir together panko and ½ cup Parmesan.
6. Sprinkle cutlets with flour. 
7. Working with one at a time, dip cutlets into eggs to coat, then dredge in panko mixture, pressing crumbs to adhere. 
8. Transfer to a plate. 
9. Let chicken stand 15 minutes.
10. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
11. Cook chicken, turning once halfway through, until seared and golden, about 4 minutes.
12. Transfer chicken to a 9x13-inch baking dish. 
13. Top each cutlet with ⅓ cup marinara sauce. 
14. Sprinkle with mozzarella, provolone, basil, and remaining ¼ cup Parmesan.
15. Bake 10 to 15 minutes, or until an instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest parts registers 165°F.
