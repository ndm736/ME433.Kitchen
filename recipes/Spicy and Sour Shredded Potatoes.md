# Spicy and Sour Shredded Potatoes

## Introduction

Hot and sour shredded potatoes is a classic Chinese home-style dish. It is simple to make and goes very well with rice.

## Ingredients

- 2 potatoes (shredded)
- Dried chili peppers (to taste)
- Minced garlic (to taste)
- Chopped green onions (to taste)
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- Salt (to taste)
- Cooking oil (as needed)

---

## Steps

1. Wash and peel the potatoes, then cut them into thin strips. Rinse with water to remove excess starch and drain well.  
2. Heat oil in a pan. Add dried chili peppers and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.  
3. Add the shredded potatoes and stir-fry over high heat until slightly softened.  
4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce, and salt. Mix well.  
5. Add chopped green onions and continue stir-frying evenly.  
6. Stir quickly and remove from heat. Serve immediately.

---

## Tips

- Rinse the potatoes to remove starch so they don’t stick together  
- Use high heat for a better texture  
- Add vinegar at the end for stronger flavor
