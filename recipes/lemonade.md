### Ingredients

- 0.5 glass water
- 2 Fresh limes
- 1 tsp Sugar
- few mint leaves
- chat masala
- ice cubes
- 0.5 glass club soda

### Directions:
- in a few tbsp add tiny hot water and mix in sugar
- Add ice cubes, club soda, and water
- Squeeze in limes
- Roll and cut mint leaves and add them
- Add chat masala and mix
- Enjoy!
