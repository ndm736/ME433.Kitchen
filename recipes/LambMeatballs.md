## Lamb Meatballs with Pomegranate Molasses
<!---
github usr: yhnli
-->
Easy to make meatballs. Can substitute with any kind of ground meat and cheese.

### Ingredients
- 500 g ground/minced lamb
- 100 g feta (cut into cubes) 
- 2 tbsp thyme 
- Medium bunch parsley (finely chopped)
- 2 or 3 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp bread crumbs
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp pomegranate molasses (optional)
- Salt and pepper for seasoning

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400F. 
2. Add minced lamb to a large bowl and add spices and seasonings. Mix well.
3. Add feta and gently mix until evenly divided.
4. Make 18 balls around 35 g each.
5. Heat olive oil in large frying pan on medium high heat.
6. Add meatballs and cook until browned on all sides
7. Transfer meatballs to a baking tray lined with baking paper.
8. Drizzle pomegranate molasses over meatballs and place in oven.
9. Bake in oven for 8 minutes or until cooked through.

Enjoy!
